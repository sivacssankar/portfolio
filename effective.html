Effective Road Accident & Alert System

Road accidents are a major cause of fatalities worldwide, often due to delayed emergency response. The Effective Road Accident and Alert System is designed to detect accidents in real time and send immediate alerts to emergency services. Using IoT sensors, GPS, and AI-based analysis, the system identifies collisions and abnormal vehicle behavior. Upon detection, it automatically notifies emergency responders, nearby hospitals, and authorities with the exact location, ensuring a faster response. This system enhances road safety, reduces fatalities, and improves emergency response efficiency.
