Fake News Detection

Abstract

The rise of fake news has become a significant problem, influencing public opinion and spreading misinformation. Fake news detection systems leverage machine learning and natural language processing (NLP) techniques to identify and classify misleading or false information.

This project focuses on developing an intelligent system that analyzes news articles and social media content to determine their authenticity.
